Lunch Partners

How does this work?

Walking out of the Capitol Theatre you find yourself in the beautiful Capitol Piazza. On the Ground Floor and on Basement 2, you'll find our lunch partner restaurants who will serve you any of the incredible lunch choices described below during our conference days. You can pay for it using the tokens stored on your wristband. Everybody starts with 120 of those tokens and you can top up more at any time at our registration desk.
